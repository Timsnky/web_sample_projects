Overview

Cytonn Technologies is the technology affiliate of Cytonn Investments Management Limited.

Cytonn Investments Management Limited provides alternative investment solutions in real estate, private equity and structured products to individual high-net-worth investors, global and local institutions and Kenyans in the diaspora. Cytonn Investments Managements Limited is an independent investment management firm. The firm is run by a team of leading professionals with over 60 years of collective experience spanning across investments, finance, legal and real estate, in both local and global markets.

Cytonn Investments has technology as one of its pillars for providing unparalleled services. With its focus on developing and deploying innovative solutions, it prides itself in a business culture that is both progressive and unparalleled in the emerging market in which it operates. Cytonn Technologies was formed to meet the technology demand that is evident in the vibrant and fast growing global and regional economy and in particular Kenya.
