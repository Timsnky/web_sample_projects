Solutions

Cytonn Technologies focuses on providing solutions in web application development, IT support and maintenance services. Our services include:

  1. Web application development

    Cytonn Technologies designs, develops, hosts and maintains web applications for its clients. Maintenance involves ensuring the website is up, updated and accessible all the time. Cytonn Technologies will maintains websites for a set number of hours at a fixed agreed rate. Where more hours are required, an hourly rate will be negotiated. Design and development is charged at an agreed hourly rate with the time and materials approach being most desired.

  2. Web hosting

    Cytonn Technologies resells performant cloud based hosting services that include server management and oversight. Web hosting is charged at a fixed monthly fee for a basic web application and can be scaled appropriately for increased needs. Cytonn resells the same type and level of hosting solutions on which it hosts its business systems.

  3. Mobile application development

    Cytonn Technologies develops Android and iOS applications for its clients. These range from simple stand-alone applications to integrated applications on client existing systems. Given the complexity of such systems, they are costed following a deep understanding of client needs.

  4. User interface design

    Cytonn Technologies designs clean and functional user interfaces for web and mobile applications. Clients are provided with intuitive and attractive interfaces that ensure users get the best experience from a website or application. We follow standard design processes where clients are involved and provide early feedback in the design.

  5. Business systems design

    Cytonn Technologies designs and develops business systems for all kinds of office and process automation. The systems are robust and scalable to meet client needs for a streamlined business operation. Business solutions are conceived, built and tested in-house to ensure reliability. The design process involves an inter-disciplinary team with experience in various business environments.

  6. Domain registration and maintenance

    We manage all the complexities of registering and maintaining domain names on behalf of our clients while ensuring websites and emails are working all the time. We undertake domain renewals and advice on methods of guarding against domain squatting and options for a variety of DNS services.

  7. Email hosting, maintenance and support

    We offer email hosting, maintenance and support services that enable our clients to concentrate on their core business. We provide clients with ready support for their email systems and help to streamline their internal and external communications as needed.

  8. Server management

    Our server experts ensure client servers are up and running while maintaining optimum server uptime. We provide services from installation, configuration, security and maintenance.
